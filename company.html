BY KIY

        Founded in April of 2011, ByKiy's up and coming clothing line has been making waves in the world of hip hop by incorporating 90s fashion styles back in clothing. CEO Nickwon Arvinger, aka Kool Kiy, created the Originals clothing line with hopes of bringing a vibrant and cultured style into the new world of fashion. ByKiy pieces have been seen on famous rappers, celebrities, and even throughout college campuses. With his headquarters located in Jersey City, New Jersey, Nickwon designs, manufactures in state, and ships each and every product to his customers. Aside from the online purchase, official Originals products will be available in pop-up shops across New Jersey as well as on college campus tours. The BiyKiy brand aims to continue providing exceptional service while becoming a reputable company with hopes of eventually becoming a household name.
